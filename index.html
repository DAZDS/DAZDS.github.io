Firm profile

The practice operates in capital-intensive industrial environments where schedule integrity, cost performance, contractual compliance and operational readiness are critical to project success. Advisory assignments focus on structured project governance, baseline control, integrated cost and schedule management, quantitative risk analysis, and defensibility of delivery data throughout engineering, procurement, construction and commissioning phases.
