Firm profile

The practice operates in capital-intensive industrial environments where schedule integrity, cost performance, contractual compliance and operational readiness are critical to project success. Advisory assignments focus on structured governance, baseline control, integrated cost & schedule management, quantitative risk analysis, and defensibility of delivery data throughout engineering, procurement, construction and commissioning phases.

Engagement model

Four-step structure designed for clarity, accountability and defensible delivery data.

1) Executive framing
Objectives, success criteria, governance and contractual context.
2) Baseline & controls
Schedule/cost/resource baseline validation, procedures, reporting lines.
3) Performance assurance
Variance analysis, EVM indicators, risk exposure, change control discipline.
4) Turnover readiness
Critical milestones, interfaces, commissioning readiness, formal handover.
Deliverables are structured for auditability, dispute readiness, and executive decision-making.
